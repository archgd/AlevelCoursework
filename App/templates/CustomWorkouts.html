Welcome to Custom Workouts

To create a custom workout just start by adding exercises from the drop-down menu, please enter the amount of reps you have completed, the weight they were completed at, the date it was completed and your email

Custom workouts let you tailor your workouts to you. If you are new to the gym we recommend using pre-planned workouts. If you would like to record a run, swim, cycle or walk please use the outdoor workouts section
